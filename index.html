The Bonfire SL1/BL4 Guidelines

~ Comprehensive, complete ruleset for SL1 and BL4 runs ~

When Soulsborne players talk about an 'SL1 run' or a 'BL4 run', they mean 'Soul Level 1' or 'Blood Level 4' runs, in which the player starts the game with the Deprived, Waste of Skin or Royalty class. These are the only starting classes that allows the player to be at SL1, then complete the game without leveling up.

These runs have a long history in the community, with a multitude of challenges created from them. In this page you can read general info on SL1 Runs, the proof you need to submit in the Bonfire Discord and banned strategies for Legendary SL1 challenges.

The base for this repository was provided by Sen#1775 over on the Demon's Souls Discord. This page exists only because of the hard work of Sen and the moderation team over there so please check them out!

Global Guidelines

  • Class -> Deprived, Waste of Skin or Royalty

    • No other class shall be used. These are the only classes in the games that start SL1, and as such these must be the classes selected by the player.

  • Champion SUbmissions

    • All Champion runs for every game must include screenshots or video of your SL1 stats and boss arenas or souls.

  • Legend Submissions

    • All Legend runs for every game must include recordings of all boss fights from when you enter the fog gat to when the boss dies.

  • All bosses must be slain!

    • Every boss must be killed. A boss can be defined by the sight of a health bar and/or a fog gate (regardless of the need to enter through such).

    Make sure to fully read the section you're interested in doing before starting your run!

  • No exploits will be tolerated!

    • There's multiple cheese tactics or exploits in the Soulsborne games. Any level skips, glitches that break boss AI or glitches that change the fight to what it's not intended as are not tolerated. Exceptions may be made on a case-by-case basis. It comes down to the discretion of the moderation team. None are allowed. Don't cheat yourself.

    Important! In terms of glitches, it is generally allowed to dupe or spawn in items as long as it simply cuts down farming time and the items can be farmed by the character, however, please notify the Bonfire Discord moderators if you want to get your run verified.

  • No help allowed!

    • Killing bosses via summoning players or with the help of NPCs is not acceptable. Every run found breaching this rule will be disqualified.

    Important! In terms of trading, it is generally allowed to get items dropped as long as it simply cuts down farming time and the items can be farmed by the character, however, please notify the Bonfire Discord moderators if you want to get your run verified.

  • No cheating. Come on!

    • Multiple "interesting tactics" have been already tried and cut down. The entire point of a challenge run is to challenge yourself. This directly combats that.

    It is not allowed to obtain items before it is usually possible. No debug. No scripts nor external tools.

    - Turning on iframes
    - Script-killing bosses via flags
    - Messing with stats
    - Cheesing the AI...


    ... and so on will result in a disqualification of the run in question, with further moderation action to be taken.

    Demon's Souls Guidelines

  • Slayer of Demons Requirements

    • For SL1 Slayer of Demons we require all bosses to be defeated with proof of the following:
    - Your stats
    - All boss souls, archstones or empty arenas.
    If you used any boss souls for items, show them as well for proof. It is strongly recommended to not use boss souls just for souls, as you are effectively destroying your own SL1 proof.

    Important! - Vanguard: For a SL1 Slayer of Demons run, you are allowed to kill Vanguard either in the tutorial or in 4-1.

  • Acceptable SL1 Monumental Criteria

    • Show your stats, rings / equipment screen before or after killing the bosses. It is recommended to show souls obtained, especially for higher NG kills.

    - SL1 NG+5

    - SL1 +0 Weapons, Normal Upgrade Path (No Infusions), No Poison, No Plague

    - SL1 Broken Weapon

    - SL1 No Roll/Block/Parry

    - SL1 Deathless
    Record the full run.
    Ignores dying to Journey to the Nexus’ Dragon God.

    - SL1 No Hit
    Record the full run.
    Ignores dying to Journey to the Nexus’ Dragon God.
    Ignores fall damage.

    Important! - Vanguard: For a SL1 Monumental run, it is recommended to kill Vanguard during the tutorial due to it being treated as a proper boss fight, offering the highest level of control for the runner. However, kills in 4-1 are allowed. In any case, both recordings must start before engaging Vanguard.

    Important! - Throwables: There is no specific number or ban on using throwables, such as throwing knives or firebombs. However, depending on how you use those, you might end up disqualified. While we allow runners to use throwables, spamming is strictly forbidden and considered under the 'No Cheesing' rule, and it is a combination of tactical item usage and proper fighting against the boss what will be considered valid. The boss that generates this situation the most is Phalanx. Firebombs are allowed on Phalanx, however, killing a majority of hoplites, or even the boss via spamming firebombs and barely engaging the boss itself will result in a clear disqualification and the need for a redo. As previously said, there is no specific max number of firebombs, but this paragraph should help runners understand that it must not be high.

  • Monumental Banned Equipment & Strategies

    • With great challenges come responsibilities and challenge control. The following items, spells or strategies are banned, and using them will result in a disqualification. If you have any doubts, ask the Bonfire Discord moderators.

    - Morion Blade and/or Clever Rat Ring on regular NG.
    Allowed on NG+5.

    - Second Chance.

    - Thief's Ring and/or Cloak against any boss.

    - Offensive spells/miracles.

    - Defensive spells such as:
    Protection, which trivializes challenges with a 30% defense increase.
    Warding, which cheaply trivializes challenges with a 70% defense increase.
    Water veil, which cheaply trivializes fire-based challenges with a 45% defense increase.

    Red Tear Stone Ring and Power Within are allowed versus Four Kings.

     - SL1 Torch only
    Kill Iudex in NG+

     - SL1 Soldering Iron only.

    It is recommended that Deacons, Halflight and Gravetender be fought under different legend restrictions.

     - SL1 Fist only

    - SL1 Perma-Death, All Bosses.
    Record the full run.

  • Legend Banned Equipment & Strategies

    • - Prisoner Chain + Lloyd's Shield Ring Combination is banned for all runs.
    - Tears of Denial. Promotes error-prone combat, avoiding death via equipment is already simple.
    - Morion Blade and Dark Hand; they are unobtainable at SL1.
    - Storm Ruler's weapon art against Yhorm. It’s too strong.
    - Siegward versus Yhorm is unacceptable.
    - Storyteller’s Staff against bosses. The weapon art makes boss fights way too passive with little risk.
    - Tanking ban: Kills aren't expected to be flawless, but avoid excessive tanking. You might be asked to redo the kill (at the checker's discretion).
    - Offensive spells are banned.
    - Ranged weapons like bows and crossbows are not allowed.
    - Damaging ranged consumables are not allowed. You can still use throwing knives to kill Crystal Sage's clones.
