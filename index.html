The Bonfire SL1/BL4 Guidelines

~ Comprehensive, complete ruleset for SL1 and BL4 runs ~

When Soulsborne players talk about an 'SL1 run' or a 'BL4 run', they mean 'Soul Level 1' or 'Blood Level 4' runs, in which the player starts the game with the Deprived, Waste of Skin or Royalty class, which are the only starting classes that allows the player to be at SL1, then complete the game without leveling up.

These runs have a long history in the community, with a multitude of challenges spawned from them. In this page you can read general info on SL1 Runs, the proof you need to submit in the Bonfire Discord and banned strategies for Legend SL1 challenges.

This repository was provided by Sen#1775 over on the Demon's Souls Discord. This page exists only because of the hard work of the Moderation team over there so please check them out!

Basic Rules

  • Class -> Deprived, Waste of Skin or Royalty

    • No other class shall be used. These are the only classes in the games that start SL1, and as such these must be the classes selected by the player.

  • All bosses must be slain!

    • Every boss must be killed. A boss can be defined by the sight of a health bar and/or a fog gate (regardless of the need to enter through such). The bosses are:

    - Vanguard
    - Phalanx , Tower Knight, Penetrator, False King Allant
    - Armor Spider, Flamelurker, Dragon God
    - Fool's Idol, Maneaters, Old Monk
    - Adjudicator, Old Hero, Storm King
    - Leechmonger, Dirty Colossus, Maiden Astraea
    - True King Allant

    Important! Vanguard falls under different specifications depending on which run you are attempting (Slayer, Monumental, Speedrun and so on).
    Make sure to fully read the section you're interested in doing before starting your run!

  • No exploits will be tolerated!

    • There's multiple cheese tactics or exploits in the Soulsborne games. Any level skips, glitches that break boss AI or glitches that change the fight to what it's not intended as are not tolerated. None are allowed. Don't cheat yourself.

    Important! In terms of glitches, it is generally OK to dupe or spawn in items as long as it simply cuts down farming time and the items can be farmed by the character, however, please notify the Bonfire Discord moderators if you want to get your run verified.

  • No help allowed!

    • Killing bosses via summoning players or with the help of Biorr is not acceptable. Every run found breaching this will be disqualified.

    Important! In terms of trading, it is generally OK to get items dropped as long as it simply cuts down farming time and the items can be farmed by the character, however, please notify the Bonfire Discord moderators if you want to get your run verified.

  • No cheating. Come on!

    • Multiple "interesting tactics" have been already tried and cut down. Needless to say, but this is a run where you challenge yourself.

    It is not allowed to obtain items before it is usually possible. No debug. No scripts nor external tools.

    - Turning on iframes
    - Script-killing bosses via flags
    - Messing with stats
    - Cheesing the AI...


    ... and so on will result in a disqualification of the run in question, with further moderation action to be taken.

    Demon's Souls Guidelines

    The SL1 Champion awards are given to those that complete a standard SL1 run. This is a run with no other stipulations than the ones listed above, where the player provides the proof listed below.

    You have two choices in order to show proof for a SL1 Slayer of Demons run:

    - Record a video showing your character stats and all boss souls, archstones or empty arenas. This will prove you defeated each of them.

    - Make a gallery of full screenshots (no cropping or editing will be allowed) of your character stats and all boss souls, archstones or empty arenas. This will prove you defeated each of them.

    Forward the record/screenshotted proof to a moderator in order to submit the run.

    Important! - Vanguard: For a SL1 Slayer of Demons run, you are allowed to kill Vanguard either in the tutorial or in 4-1.

    If you used any boss souls for items, show them as well for proof. It is strongly recommended to not use boss souls just for souls, as you are effectively destroying your own SL1 proof. For boss weapons, if you use Storm King's soul for Morion Blade, you will need to give Flamelurker's soul to Blacksmith Ed. In this case, alongside the rest of your proof must come proof of having Morion Blade, and Flamelurker and Storm King's empty arenas and/or unlocked archstones.

    Dark Souls II Guidelines

    You can obtain a special role if you are the fastest player in the server to complete a SL1 run. However, if someone beats your time, you will lose the role.

    - Time of completion will be dictated by RTA (Real-Time Attack). Experimentation has found IGT (Ingame Time) to be inconsistent, hard to verify, and forces PS3 runners to be on the Private Server in order to provide a full HH:MM:SS time.

    - Due to this, it is recommended to use a split tool, and provide an on-screen timer during the run.

    - A speedrun will start on the tutorial. Recording should start when you gain control of your character. Vanguard is mandatory. Death by tutorial Dragon God is mandatory. No skips allowed, no glitches.

    - Emulation, PS3 and PS5: RPCS3 and PS3 will be categorized differently due to FPS and hardware differences and obviously, the Remake will be its own category.
